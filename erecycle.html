Where do your electronics go?

It's not just paper, plastic and metal that can be recycled. Your electronics (small and large) have valuable components that can also be recycled. In addition, these electronics often have toxic substances that must be handled properly. It is your responsibility to make sure your electronics don't just end up in a landfill!


What kind of small electronics can you recycle?
  • Batteries
  • Compact Fluorescent Lightbulbs
  • CDs and DVDs
  • Cell phones
  • Calculators
  • USB Drives
  • MP3 players
What kind of large electronics can you recycle?
  • Computers
  • Projectors
  • Televisions
  • Refrigerators
Where do I bring my recyclables?
Every building manager should have a small container in their office for small electronics. Talk to your building manager directly about the disposal of large electronics.
